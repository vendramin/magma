Let $G=\mathbb{Z}/24$, $H=\langle 4\rangle$ and 
$K=\langle 6\rangle$. Then $H+K\simeq C_{12}$ and 
$H\cap K\simeq C_{2}$: 
```
> R := ResidueClassRing(24);;
> G := AdditiveGroup(R);
> H := sub<G|4*G.1>;
> K := sub<G|6*G.1>;
> GroupName(H+K);
C12
> GroupName(H meet K);
C2
```
We can indeed check that $H+K=\langle 2\rangle$ and 
$H\cap K=\langle 12\rangle$: 
```
> H+K eq sub<G|2*G.1>;
true
> H meet K eq sub<G|12*G.1>;
true    
```
